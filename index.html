Who We Are

Menekeh Research Group MRG is a Liberian led research and consulting organization supporting evidence based decision making accountability and inclusive development. MRG works with communities civil society organizations government institutions and partners to generate ethical and practical research that strengthens policies and programs.

What We Do

  • Research and community surveys
  • Monitoring and evaluation
  • Training and capacity building
  • Policy analysis and advisory services
