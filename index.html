Who We Are

Menekeh Research Hub (MRH) is a Liberian-led research and development consulting organization supporting evidence-based decision making, accountability, and inclusive development. MRH works with communities, civil society organizations, government institutions, and development partners to generate ethical, practical, and policy-relevant research that strengthens programs and public outcomes.

What We Do

  • Research and community-based surveys
  • Monitoring, evaluation, and learning (MEL)
  • Training and institutional capacity building
  • Policy analysis and governance advisory services
  • Digital communication and data-driven outreach
