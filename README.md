# Menekeh Research Hub (MRH)

Menekeh Research Hub (MRH) is a Liberian-led research and development consulting organization committed to evidence-based decision making, governance, accountability, and inclusive development.

## What We Do
- Research and community-based surveys  
- Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL)  
- Training and institutional capacity building  
- Policy analysis and governance advisory services  
- Digital communication and data-driven outreach
